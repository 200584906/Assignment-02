Fast & furious

Fast & Furious is an American media franchise centered on series
of action films that are largely concerned with street racing,
heists, spies, and family. The franchise also includes short films,
a television series, toys, video games, live shows, and theme park
attractions.The films are distributed by Universal Pictures.
